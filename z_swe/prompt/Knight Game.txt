## Turn 1: The foundation logic of the game

Create an infinite round game called Knight_CLI using plain JavaScript, no framework.
Every round, the player has to fight a monster.
The CLI must be clear on every user's interaction. Clear Result of previous action must be shown.
The CLI must utilize horizontal space, avoid vertical scrolling as much as possible.
The player can: Attack (use weapon in hands to attack, if no weapon, use bare hands), Calm (heal Stamina).
The player has the following stats to be provided when fighting:
HP, Armor, Stamina, Mana, Damage for `Attack` action.
Player based stats: HP = 20, Armor = 0, Stamina = 100, Mana = 10, Base Damage = 2.
Monster based stats: HP = 10, Armor = 0, Stamina = 100, Mana = 10, Base Damage = 2.
Damage to be dealt = (Base Damage + Weapon Damage).
After every round, the player can: buy new armor or weapon
(there are 4 piece of armors of the same tier: helmet, chestplate, leggings, and boots,
only when user buy all 4 armor, the shop will renew with the next tier both armor and weapon.
Armor and weapon have infinite tier. Price for higher tier must be calculated mild exponentially (to the power of 1.5)),
upgrade their stats (upgrade on level up 5 skill points, getting XP via win against the monster, specifically include:
strength (+ base damage. The more strength, the more stamina waste when trigger `Attack` action. when
Stamin runs out, the user is forced to perform `Calm` with clear message), dexterity (+ critical chance
(x2 damage), increase hit chance), agility (increase dodge chance), intelligence (+ Mana, increase Stamina and Mana heal
when Calm action), defense (+ Health)), proceed next round.
All monsters stats are based on the skill points,
for every round, monster have +2 more skill points. On level % 10 == 2, monster has full strength,
== 4 has full dexterity, == 6 has full agility, == 8 has full defense, == 10 has full Intelligence.
After every 10 rounds, the base stats of monster will increase by 20%, and armor will also increase +20.
Armor mechanism: similar to Health, will decrease when enemy attacks. When armor runs out, health will decrease.
Magical attack bypassed armor.
The mana can be used by both Human and Monster as follows:
1. Lightning Stunt enemy for 1 turn, cost 80% of max mana, damage = 20% of mana used.
2. Contaminate Poison enemy for the rest of turn, cost 80% of max mana, damage = 2% of mana used every human turn.
3. Weakness curse enemy for the next turn, cost 35% of max mana, enemy gets all stats debuffed by 35% each.
The menu of actions after fighting must use number to navigate. Player can choose `Back` at anytime, except
proceeding to next Round. 
Ensure DRY and SOLID principles.
The folder to be implemented is Knight_CLI inside z_swe folder.
